Hyder Finer Foods Inc. is an enterprise leader in South Asian and Middle Eastern Foods. We have built a strong reputation through our competitive pricing, quality products, in stock bulk items, rapid delivery, personal services and up-to-date certifications. Hyder Finer Foods has grown from a wholesale food distributer in Illinois, to one of North America's leading ethnic food distributors. Hyder Finer Foods Inc., together with its sister Distributors, Moghul Foods LLC and New Royal Distributors Inc. of Midwest - make up one of USA's largest importers and distributors of Ethnic Foods .Our extensive experience and market knowledge allow us to source large volumes at competitive rates, bringing greater savings to the customer. We welcome you to start a new journey with us, and experience your brand flourish.

HFF deliver operational excellence in sales force, customer service, streamlined warehouse logistics, and efficient management practices.Illinois is our largest office where we have 5 delivery vehicles and a 96,000 sq. ft warehouse., which includes more than 50,000 cu. ft. of freezer and cooler space, allowing for incredible growth opportunity, both for us as well as for your Brand.
